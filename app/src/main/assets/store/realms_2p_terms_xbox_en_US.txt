Realms is a subscription for a personal multiplayer server to be used with supported versions of Minecraft (game sold separately) on Xbox and other supported platforms on which you own the game (Minecraft Java Edition and Xbox360 not supported). Xbox Live Gold subscription is required to play on Xbox (sold separately). You can cancel your subscription by accessing Services & subscriptions in your Microsoft Account. If you play Minecraft through GamePass, Realms is a separate subscription. You will need to purchase and cancel each subscription separately. If your subscription is inactive for more than 18 months, your Realms data will be deleted from the server. ISP fees may apply. Realms discounts cannot be combined with other offers and are not redeemable for cash; Requirements, features, online services, or subscription subject to change and/or retirement.
